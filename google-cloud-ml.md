In order to run training and prediction jobs in Google Cloud you require
a Google Cloud Platform Project along with access to a Storage Bucket
and access to Compute resources.

The easiest way to start your GCP account is to open the Cloud Platform
(Web) Console: <https://console.cloud.google.com/>. If you already have
a Google account and you already have payment details attached, then
sign in with this account. If you already have a Google account and will
be able to add payment details then sign in with this account and add
your payment details.

Otherwise create a new Google account and ensure you have some payment
details to hand.

The payment details are required because the “Always Free” teir of
Google Cloud has certain restrictions and requires a valid account for
overage charges. For this course we don’t expect to spend more than a
couple of pounds. If you have free credit from signing up you will be
able to use that but a valid payment account is still required.

Pricing for ML Compute:
<https://cloud.google.com/ml-engine/docs/pricing>

Pricing for Storage: <https://cloud.google.com/storage/pricing>

For details on creating a Billing account see:
<https://cloud.google.com/billing/docs/how-to/manage-billing-account>
